About the Author

As an anthropology student, my goal is to partake in the dissemination of knowledge and information to people that have little to no awareness regarding primates. Publishing a website with the use of an application called, “Mobirise” seems plausible as it is the most feasible way to increase people’s knowledge regarding primates’ characteristics, behavior, and adaptations in the environment.
